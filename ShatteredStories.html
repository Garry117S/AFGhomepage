Become the hero you were (not) trained to be. Join the Asset Recovery Team. ​You'll save the day, get paid, and probably question all your life choices. ​(Seriously, no experience required.)

About This Game You've always been a misfit on the Ishihara Station, a bustling hub in the vast emptiness of space. You've tried every dead-end job, but nothing has ever stuck. That all changes when a new business opens its doors. ​The Asset Recovery Team. ​The job posting promises a life of adventure, high-stakes missions to rescue important people, and most importantly getting paid big time. You figure with a little training, you could be the hero you've always wanted to be. ​The catch? There's no training, your combat suit rental fees eat up most of your profits, and your team is anything but professional. Now, you'll have to rely on your wits to survive and get the job done. ​Key Features ​Explore Fractured Worlds: Traverse haunting landscapes and crumbling ruins, from forgotten forests to desolate wastelands. ​Dynamic Encounters: Face off against mysterious creatures and challenging enemies with unique abilities and attack patterns. ​Character Progression: Upgrade your weapons and armour, and unlock new skills as you grow stronger and more experienced. Mature Content Description The developers describe the content like this: Contains violence, player character may be attacked by enemies and can attack enemies. Player may receive quests to kill bandits or other enemies. System Requirements Minimum: OS: 11 Processor: 7800x3d Memory: 48 GB RAM Graphics: 6950xt Storage: 100 GB available space Sound Card: DirectX compatible VR Support: Not supported
